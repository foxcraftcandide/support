Frequently asked questions

How do I reset my password?
Open the app → Login → Forgot Password. Enter your account email and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. Check spam/junk if you don’t see the email within a few minutes.
How do I contact support?
Use the “Email support” button above or write to f36823@gmail.com with details about your device, OS version, app version, and a description of the issue.
My purchase/subscription isn’t recognized
Ensure you are using the same Apple ID you used for purchase. In the app, go to Settings → Restore Purchases. If that doesn’t help, contact support with your App Store receipt.
