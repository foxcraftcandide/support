Focusly

Privacy Policy • Last Updated: April 11, 2026

Introduction

Focusly is designed to help you regain control of your time by locking your phone for set durations. We believe your focus is your most valuable asset, and your privacy is equally important. This document explains how we handle your data.

Key Guarantee: Focusly does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All operations happen entirely on your device.

Prominent Disclosure: Accessibility Service

To provide its core locking functionality and prevent "bypass" attempts, Focusly requires the use of the Accessibility Service and the BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE permission.

  • How we use it: We monitor the package names of apps that come to the foreground. If a lock is active and a user attempts to leave Focusly, the service brings Focusly back to the front.
  • Data Protection: This service is used purely for real-time app switching enforcement. It does not read your screen content, capture keystrokes, or record your usage history.
  • Requirement: This permission is optional but required for "Enforced Mode" to work effectively across all apps.

Permissions & Their Purpose

  • Notification Access (DND): Used to silence incoming notifications and calls during your focus sessions to minimize distractions.
  • System Overlay (System Alert Window): Used to display a "Curtain" overlay if a user attempts to bypass the lock screen, providing a visual barrier.
  • Ignore Battery Optimizations: Used to ensure the lock timer and background services are not killed by the operating system, maintaining lock integrity.

Data Security

Focusly is built with security-first architecture. Since we do not use any external servers for data processing, there is no risk of data breaches from our side. Your focus data and settings are stored locally in secure app-private storage.

Third-Party Services

Focusly may contain advertisements served by third-party SDKs (such as AdMob). These services may collect device identifiers (like Advertising ID) to serve relevant ads. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details on their data handling practices.
