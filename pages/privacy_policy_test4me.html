Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Test For Me. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application designed to facilitate Google Play Side-by-Side testing groups.

2. Information We Collect

We collect essential information to provide a seamless testing coordination experience:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture (facilitated via Firebase Authentication).
  • Tester Group Data: Information about groups you manage or join, including application links and package names.
  • Activity Data: Feedback notes and verification status provided during the 14-day testing cycles.
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens required for real-time group updates via Firebase Cloud Messaging.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for the core functionality of the platform:

  • Managing 12+ tester groups for Google Play Console compliance.
  • Aiding in the 14-day continuous testing verification process.
  • Authenticating user access and securing tester environments.
  • Notifying you of group updates and testing feedback.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information is shared only as follows:

  • To Other Group Members: Your provided email address and app link are shared with fellow testers in the groups you join to enable Side-by-Side testing.
  • Google Firebase: We utilize Google's cloud infrastructure (Firebase) for secure database storage, hosting, and authentication.

5. Your Rights and Control

You maintain full control over your information:

  • Access & Updates: You can modify your profile details directly within the app settings.
  • Account Deletion: You may permanently delete your account and all associated data through the application at any time.
  • Transparency: You can request a summary of the data we hold at any time via our support channels.

6. Security

We prioritize the security of your data. By leveraging Google's secure cloud infrastructure, we implement robust encryption and security protocols. However, please remember that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.
