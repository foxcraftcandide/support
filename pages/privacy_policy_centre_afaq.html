Privacy Policy - Centre Afaq

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

Centre Afaq is an application that stores and manages data for educational institutions. This privacy policy explains what data the app may collect, how it is used, and how you can manage or delete it.

Data We Collect

  • Account data: names, email addresses, roles (Admin/Teacher/Student/Parent).
  • Academic data: attendance records, grades, assignments, schedules.
  • Financial data: invoices and payment records if you use the invoicing features.
  • Device and diagnostic data: error reports and usage analytics if enabled.

How Data Is Stored & Used

All application data is stored in your Firebase project (Firestore). The app does not transmit data to any service outside those you configure. If you enable analytics or crash reporting, those services may be operated by third parties (for example, Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics).

Third-Party Services

The project may integrate third-party services such as Firebase for authentication and database, Google services for storage, and optional analytics. Check your Firebase console and enabled APIs to see which external services are active for your deployment.

Data Retention & Deletion

Data retention is determined by your Firebase configuration and rules. To delete or export data, use the Firebase Console or contact your system administrator. This repository does not provide remote deletion — actions happen within your configured backend.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. This page will list the last updated date above.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the project maintainer or your institution's IT administrator.
