Privacy Policy 🎨 Coloring World

Your privacy and your child's safety are our top priorities. Learn how we protect your data and comply with COPPA.

Last Updated: April 5, 2024

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Coloring World, we take your privacy and your child's safety extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information our app collects, how we use it, and how we protect it. We are committed to complying with all applicable privacy laws and regulations, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

What We Believe In

  • Your privacy is our priority
  • We collect minimal information
  • We never sell your data
  • Children deserve protection
  • Transparency and honesty matter

1. Information We Collect

Information We DO Collect:

  • User-Created Content: Only the drawings and artwork you create within the app. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • App Usage Data: Basic usage statistics to help us improve the app (crashes, features used)
  • Language Preference: Your selected language setting to personalize your experience
  • Device Information: Basic device type information for compatibility purposes

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • ❌ Personal names or identities
  • ❌ Email addresses or phone numbers
  • ❌ Age or date of birth
  • ❌ Location data or GPS coordinates
  • ❌ Browsing history or online activity
  • ❌ Contacts or friend lists
  • ❌ Biometric data or device identifiers
  • ❌ Cookies or tracking technologies

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide and improve the coloring features
  • Technical Support: To fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Localization: To provide the app in your preferred language
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app (anonymous data only)

We DO NOT use your information for:

  • Marketing or advertising purposes
  • Selling to third parties
  • Behavioral tracking or profiling
  • Targeted advertising (especially to children)

3. Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Stays: All your artwork and data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or sync your drawings to our servers or any cloud service unless you explicitly choose to share them.

Data Protection: We use industry-standard security practices to protect any data we do collect during app usage. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

How Long We Keep Data: User-created artwork is stored on your device until you delete it. We do not retain this data on our servers.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Coloring World is a children's application, and we are fully committed to protecting children's privacy.

Important: We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old, and we encourage parental involvement in children's online activities.
  • We do not require personal information to use the app
  • We do not have user accounts or login systems
  • We do not collect or sell children's personal data
  • We do not use behavioral tracking or targeted advertising
  • We do not link to external social media or unsafe websites
  • All external links (if any) are parent-approved educational resources

5. Third-Party Services

Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous app usage data. This data does not identify individual users and is used only to improve the app.

Gallery Saving: When you save artwork to your device gallery, you're using your device's built-in storage system. We do not access or store this data.

File Sharing: When you choose to share artwork, you're using your device's sharing functionality. We do not store or monitor shared content.

6. Permissions We Request

Storage Permission

Why we need it: To save your artwork to your device's local storage so you can access it later or share it.

What we access: Only the app's specific storage directory. We do not access other files or personal data on your device.

7. Parental Controls & Inquiries

Parents or guardians: If you have questions about your child's use of this app or our privacy practices, please contact us.

Right to Access and Delete: Parents/guardians can request to see what data we have about their child or request deletion of any data. Since we don't collect personal information, there's nothing to delete, but we welcome your inquiries.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the app constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

9. Advertisements & In-App Purchases

Ads: This app is completely ad-free. We do not display any advertisements, especially not targeted ads to children.

In-App Purchases: There are no in-app purchases. The app is free to use with all features available.

10. GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Union, the app complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • We minimize data collection
  • We provide transparency about our practices
  • You have the right to access and delete your data
  • We have legitimate grounds for any data processing
