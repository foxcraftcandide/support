1 Overview

Hifd Quran ("the App", "we", "our") is a free, open-source Quran memorization application developed for Android and iOS. This Privacy Policy explains what information — if any — is collected when you use the App and how it is handled.

Short version: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App is fully offline and requires no account or registration.

2 Information We Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to a server
  • Contacts, camera, or microphone data

All user preferences — such as your memorization plan, progress, selected language, font settings, and review day — are stored locally on your device using Android/iOS SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

3 Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data only on your device:

  • Memorization plan type and duration
  • Current task index and overall progress
  • Daily streak counter
  • Weekly review day preference
  • Ramadan mode setting
  • Selected Quran edition / language
  • Font family and font size preferences
  • Dark mode preference

You can delete all of this data at any time by using the "Reset Plan" option in Settings, or by uninstalling the App.

4 Internet & Permissions

The App does not require an internet connection to function. All Quran text, translations, and fonts are bundled within the App at install time.

The App does not request the following sensitive Android/iOS permissions:

  • Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)
  • Camera (CAMERA)
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)
  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS)
  • Phone state (READ_PHONE_STATE)

5 Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services that collect data, including:

  • No Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics
  • No Facebook SDK
  • No advertising networks (AdMob, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services (Sentry, Crashlytics, etc.)

The App is distributed via the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain technical information as part of the distribution process — please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

6 Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Because we collect no personal information whatsoever, there is nothing to disclose under COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) or similar regulations.

7 Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your operating system up to date to ensure your data remains protected.

8 Open Source

The source code of Hifd Quran is open source and licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International License. You can inspect the full codebase to verify these privacy claims independently.

Source code is available on GitHub. The Quran text and translations used in the App are in the public domain or licensed under permissive open licenses.
